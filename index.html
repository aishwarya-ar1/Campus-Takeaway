Campus Takeaway




Our Objective:

  1. The customer can now place a prepaid order on the CampusTakeaway App, and walk into the restaurant to pick up the order at the pre-decided time.

  2. We are doubling down on our efforts to keep team members and customers safe by introducing Contactless Takeaway.

  3. The new service is aimed at providing a completely cashless and contactless experience to consumers.

  4. We have upped our existing operational protocols with its 4X Safety promise.

  5. The delivery teams wash and sanitise their hands and bags after every order.

  6. All team members, including delivery riders, are regularly screened and undergo daily temperature checks, wear masks and gloves.
